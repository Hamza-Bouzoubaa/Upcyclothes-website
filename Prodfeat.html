How We Operate

Level 0

This level consists of fabrics and clothes that have been lightly used and are in a condition to be sold.

Level 1

This level consists of all those fabrics and clothes that are lightly to moderately used and are in a condition to be sold. These articles will most likely be given to thrift stores and small businesses.

Level 2

This level consists of everything beyond the point of repair, fabric that cannot be used for upcycling, nearing the waste level. These fabrics are used for many other uses that don't necessarily involve making new clothes. A few of these uses include: Accessories, Toys and Filling bean bags and mattresses

What can you do?

We offer a quick and easy solution to upcycle your clothes. Along with the assurance that it's going to the right place i.e not being dumped in a landfill or worse, burnt.
We plan to accomplish that with our unique 3 level system:
1. We collect clothes, easing the process of people who are trying to do the right thing,
2. We sort them into three levels of usability,
3. We keep clothes that are unsusable in their current state
4. Transport the rest to certified organizations who upcycle clothes and restore them, ensuring that they share our values, and vision.
