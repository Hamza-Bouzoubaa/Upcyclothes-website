Overall Look and Feel of Upcyclothes
===============================

The overall look and feel is hopeful, calming, and renewal. 
The website should be calm and gentle for the user but inspiring enough to keep them interested.

Colours
========
For Upcyclothes, colours reflect our vision to the users and represent that we come from a place of passion and sincerity.
These are our color swatches:
1. Green
- #097969 rgb(31,160,96)
2. Red
- #f44336 
3. Black
- #000000 rgb(0,0,0)
4. White
- #ffffff 
4-Grey
-#555

Iconography
===========
The large focus will be a refreshing and simple design, so it is a better experience for users.
Simple and relatable.

Typography
==========
We will be using serif from Google Fonts.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be designed to be simplistic and easy to use.
