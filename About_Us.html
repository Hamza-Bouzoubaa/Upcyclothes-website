About Us

Origins

Have you ever thought about your wasted clothes? Where does it all go? Well we dug deep and the results might be shocking. 80% of clothes are wasted (see:https://www.roadrunnerwm.com/blog/textile-waste-environmental-crisis) and landfills and pollution continue to grow.

Why?

It is for this exact reason that we started Upcyclothes. Upcyclothes is the one stop solution to all your discarded clothes.
